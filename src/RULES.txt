gECRET HITLER RULES IN A NUTSHELL:
Game size:
8(5 Liberal, 2 Fascist, 1 Hitler)

11 Fascist cards
6 Liberal cards

Affiliation(Liberal, Fascist) and Secret roles(Liberal, 
fascist, hitler) assigned at random

Objectives to win

Liberal
5 Liberal policies played
Kill hitler through fasist kill power

Fascist
hitler elected chancelor AFTER 3 fascist policies played
6 fascist policies

Gameplay loop
{ 

	President shifts to the next player alphabetically by name(randomly set at onset)
	president select a chancelor to vote for
	Vote for pair(Ja!, Nein!) (draws mean a concensus of Nein!)

	If concensus is Ja!(YES!){
		president draws 3 policies from deck(secret)
		chancelor is given 2(secret) policies from pres, picks 1, is played
		fasict power is activated, if able
		discussion...(can happen at any point really)
	} 
	else (NEIN!){
		if president chancelor pair rejected x3 in a row {
        		HUNG PARLAMENT: random policy from top of deck is played(FASICT POWERS DO_NOT_ACTIVATE)
        	}
	}
}

//--------
Fascist Powers: Current President gets to use (activated when a fasist policy is played by the chancelor, NOT BY HUNG PARLEMENT)
At 1 policy:
Nothing

At 2 policies:
Investigate a player, you see their party affiliation(liberal, fascist)

At 3 policies(NOTE, FASCISTS WIN IF HITLER ELECTED CHANCELOR _AFTER_ THIS POINT)
Rigged election: current president gets to choose the next president. Chancelor selection and voting proceeds as normal after

At 4 policies
Kill a player: player is removed from gameplay. can still hang around and win with their team

At 5 policies
Kill a player
